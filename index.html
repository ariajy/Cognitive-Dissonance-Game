What this game is

Dissonant Choices is a short narrative simulation about cognitive dissonance. Each scene asks you to choose an action, then reflect on how that action fits your beliefs.

How to play

Start a scene, pick one option, and continue through strain, coping, and summary screens. Your current strain level and highlighted beliefs update as your decisions accumulate.

How to read feedback

Strain is shown as Low, Medium, or High (based on your current tension). A smaller percentage next to it is a fine-grained read—the level name is what to notice first.

Belief visuals show direction and size of change. Up/down indicators and values are provided together so you can compare outcomes without relying on color.

Tutorial and settings

You can replay the tutorial from Settings at any time. Restart clears the current save state and starts a fresh run.
